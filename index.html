Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Welcome to Cosmos Reposter. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data within our app.

1. Information We Collect

Cosmos Reposter may request access to limited user data provided by Snapchat through the official Snapchat APIs, such as your display name or profile information. We do not collect any additional personal data beyond what is required for authentication and content sharing.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used solely to enable login functionality and allow you to share your own content from Cosmos Reposter to Snapchat. We never sell, share, or misuse your information.

3. Data Storage

No personal data is permanently stored on our servers. Any session tokens or temporary data are securely managed and automatically deleted after use.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Snapchat’s official APIs (Login Kit and Creative Kit). All data access and handling comply with Snapchat’s developer policies.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke permissions at any time through your Snapchat account settings. If you wish to have any data removed, please contact us.
